Competitive Systems

The rulebook is lean so high-skill teams can improvise faster.

We obsess over mechanical fidelity so every player can snap into flow. The arsenal, movement, and round economy reward discipline and audacity in equal measure.

Adaptive recoil

Weapons react to momentum, stance, and heat. High-accuracy fire rewards timing your bursts, not memorising spray patterns.

Momentum movement

Slide into corners, mantle low-poly geometry, and cancel momentum for tight micro-plays. No abilities—just movement mastery.

Transparent economy

Buy phases surface the intel you actually need: teammate loadouts, projected eco, and spike routes. No busywork menus.

Broadcast ready

Observer tools, sky cams, and cinematic replays help communities tell stories from scrims to LAN finals.

Map manifest

Three launch maps tuned for relentless retakes and clean sightlines.

Subway

Collapsed metro lines, vertical vents, and duel-ready hallways force quick rotations and sonic booms of crossfire.

Factory

Industrial killboxes with layered catwalks. Out-macro your opponents or dominate with ruthless anchor play.

Arena

Main stage geometry purpose-built for majors. Clean sightlines, dramatic spikesites, and every angle matters.

Round rhythm

Attackers commit to spike plants with broadcast-ready countdowns while defenders ride the tension. Overtime keeps climbing until a team secures two clean rounds.

Competitive backbone

Ranked, scrim, and tournament servers all share anti-cheat, netcode, and analytics stacks. Your grind translates to the stage.
