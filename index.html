About

Cyber League is a tactical, team-based FPS that puts skill first. No abilities, no agents just pure skill and precision. Every match is a test of strategy and teamwork in a lowpoly cyberpunk world.

Features

Precision Gameplay
Refined shooting mechanics and competitive balance.
Tactical Maps
Modular arenas designed for strategy and skillful play.
Fast-Paced Combat
Engaging matches that reward reflexes and teamwork.
Minimal Cyberpunk Aesthetic
A sleek, premium visual design with lowpoly environments.
